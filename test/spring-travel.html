Spring 2026 Travel Guide

Published March 28, 2026 — Updated weekly

Spring is the sweet spot for travel: pleasant weather, smaller crowds, and better prices than summer peak. Here are our top picks for March through May 2026.

1. Japan — Cherry Blossom Season

Best time: Late March to mid-April

Tokyo's Ueno Park and Meguro River are iconic viewing spots, with peak bloom typically March 25 - April 5. Kyoto follows a few days later. Temperatures hover around 14-20°C — perfect for walking.

Tip: Book 3+ months ahead for cherry blossom period. JR Pass saves ~40% on intercity travel.

2. Portugal — Algarve Coast

Best time: April to May

The Algarve offers 300+ days of sunshine annually. Spring temperatures of 18-24°C are ideal before the summer crowds descend. Faro is the gateway, but Lagos and Tavira are the real gems.

CityAvg tempWater tempBudget/day
Lagos21°C17°C$70-110

Tavira22°C17°C$55-90

Tip: Rent a car for coastal exploration. The EN125 road connects all major beach towns.

3. Thailand — Pre-Monsoon Window

Best time: March (before April heat peak)

March is the last comfortable month before Thailand's scorching April. Bangkok hits 35°C+ with high humidity. Better options: Chiang Mai (cooler, less crowded) or the islands (Koh Samui still dry).

Caution: Air quality in northern Thailand degrades significantly in March-April due to agricultural burning.

4. New Zealand — Autumn Colors

Best time: Late March to April

The Southern Alps put on a spectacular autumn display. Arrowtown near Queenstown is famous for its golden poplars. Temperatures: 8-18°C — bring layers.

5. Morocco — Perfect Shoulder Season

Best time: March to May

Marrakech, Fez, and the Atlas Mountains are at their best in spring. Comfortable temperatures of 20-28°C, blooming almond trees, and lower prices than peak season.
